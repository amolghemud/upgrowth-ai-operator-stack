# Plugins

Operator-grade AI plugin specifications used at upGrowth.

## Available plugins

### Research & Diagnostics
- [Research Mapper](research-mapper.md) – Structured, source-backed market and industry research
- [SEO Audit Mapper](seo-audit-mapper.md) – Actionable SEO diagnostics with prioritized fixes and opportunity sizing
- [Funnel Diagnostics](funnel-diagnostics.md) – Conversion funnel analysis to identify leaks and size revenue impact

### Strategy & Planning
- [Competitor Teardown](competitor-teardown.md) – Systematic competitive analysis with positioning and strategic recommendations
- [Campaign Planner](campaign-planner.md) – Integrated multi-channel campaign planning with measurement frameworks

### Execution
- [Content Brief Generator](content-brief-generator.md) – Strategic content briefs with search intent, competitor gaps, and outlines
- [Brand Voice Calibrator](brand-voice-calibrator.md) – Brand voice analysis and guidelines for consistent communication

## Recommended workflow

1. **Diagnose** – Run Research Mapper, SEO Audit Mapper, or Funnel Diagnostics to understand the current state
2. **Strategize** – Use Competitor Teardown and Campaign Planner to define the approach
3. **Execute** – Generate Content Briefs calibrated with Brand Voice for consistent output
